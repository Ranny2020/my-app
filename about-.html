About


The Student Industrial Work Experience Scheme (SIWES) was established by the Industrial Training Fund (ITF) in 1973.
The scheme was initiated to address the problem of inadequate practical skills among graduates of tertiary institutions in Nigeria, which was affecting their employability in industries.
The scheme was designed to expose and prepare students of universities, polytechnics, and colleges of education for the industrial work situation they would encounter after graduation.
It provides students with the opportunity to handle and manage equipment and machinery that are usually not available in their institutions.
